How to Play

Players take turns choosing a column from 1 to 7. The token drops to the lowest open space. Get four tokens in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win.

Keyboard shortcuts: press 1-7 to choose a column, R to reset, and H to open/close this help panel.

Player 1 starts.
